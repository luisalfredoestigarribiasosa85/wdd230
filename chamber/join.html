Membership Benefits

Non-Profit

As a nonprofit member of the Chamber, your business will receive:

  • Access to chamber resources and events
  • Opportunities to network with other professionals in your field
  • A voice on industry issues through our advocacy program
  • Discounted rates on chamber programs and services

Free

Bronze Membership

As a Bronze member of the Chamber, your business will receive:

  • All benefits of Nonprofit membership plus:
  • Recognition as a "Bronze Member" on the Chamber website

$150 per year

Silver Membership

As a Silver Member of the Chamber, your business will receive:

  • All benefits of Bronze and Nonprofit memberships plus:
  • Dedicated chamber staff contact throughout the year
  • "Spotlight" recognition at chamber events and on the website

$475 per year

Gold Membership

As a Gold member of the Chamber, your business will receive:

  • All benefits of Silver and Bronze memberships plus:
  • Permanent placement on the Chamber's Business Directory
  • One free advertisement in the Chamber's newsletter each quarter
