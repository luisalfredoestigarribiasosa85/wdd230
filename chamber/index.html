Get to know us more...

Nestled in the heart of Paraguay, Itauguá City is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant artisan community. Home to skilled craftsmen producing exquisite ñandutí lace, a traditional Paraguayan craft dating back centuries, Itauguá offers visitors a unique glimpse into the country's artistic traditions. Explore our bustling markets, where you can witness artisans meticulously weaving intricate lace patterns by hand, or immerse yourself in the colorful festivities of our annual ñandutí festival. Discover the magic of Itauguá, where tradition and creativity converge to create an unforgettable experience for visitors from near and far.
